About Me

I am a self taught computer programmer who loves to code, test, debug and create. I started coding when I was 15 and since then it has become an obsession for me. I have always pushed myself to learn something new and exciting. But I believe learning is just beginning. Rather implementing that skill to solve a problem or improve it, is much more important. I even like to contribute to open source projects by finding bugs, solving them(if possible for me) and translations.Presently I am persuing my Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering at R.V College of Engineering, Bangalore. Apart from coding I like to play sports like Table Tennis, Badminton, Cricket and Chess. I like to explore new areas and people from different disciplines. I am even interested in teaching "How To Code" to beginners, specially school students.
